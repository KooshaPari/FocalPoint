## 3. App Functionality & Limitations

### 3.1 What FocalPoint Does
FocalPoint is a focus management platform that:
- Stores your tasks, goals, and rules locally on your device
- Reads calendar events (with your permission) to enable schedule-aware rules
- Integrates with Canvas, Google Calendar, and GitHub (optional, user-authorized)
- Enforces app blocking policies via iOS FamilyControls
- Maintains an audit trail of rule evaluations and state changes
- Provides a reward/penalty ledger for behavior tracking

### 3.2 What FocalPoint is NOT
FocalPoint is **not** a substitute for:
- Professional medical or therapeutic advice
- Clinical mental health treatment
- A parental control system (v1 is individual-focused; parental features in v2+)
- A fitness or health tracking app (though optional health data integration is planned)
- A school attendance or grading system

**Disclaimer:** Using FocalPoint does not guarantee improved focus, better grades, or lifestyle change. Results depend on your consistent effort and adherence to your own rules.

### 3.3 Accuracy & Completeness
The App makes best efforts to:
- Accurately evaluate rules based on your configured conditions
- Sync events from external services without data loss
- Maintain audit chain integrity

However, we do not warrant that:
- Rule evaluations are 100% error-free
- External services (Canvas, Google, GitHub) always respond correctly
- Background sync is guaranteed to trigger at all times
- Calendar data is always accurate or complete

## 10. Device Management & Enforcement

### 10.1 FamilyControls Framework
FocalPoint uses Apple's FamilyControls framework to enforce screen-time restrictions:
- **Local Enforcement:** All enforcement happens on your device; policies are not synced remotely (Phase 1)
- **User Consent:** You must authorize FamilyControls access when you first use enforcement features
- **Device Restrictions:** You understand that the rules you create will restrict app access on your device
- **Bypass & Unlock:** You can spend "bypass budget" to temporarily unlock restricted apps, subject to your configured policies

### 10.2 iOS Compatibility
FamilyControls is only available on:
- iOS 16+ devices
- Apps that have obtained the FamilyControls entitlement from Apple
