# Code of Conduct

FocalPoint is committed to creating an inclusive, respectful community. All community members are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

## Our Values

- **Respect**: Treat all people with kindness and professionalism
- **Inclusion**: Welcome contributors of all backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives
- **Transparency**: Communicate openly about decisions, feedback, and concerns
- **Accountability**: Hold ourselves and each other to high standards

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing opinions
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other people

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, identity, race, religion, or sexual orientation
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning**: First-time, minor violations
2. **Temporary ban**: Repeated or serious violations (1–3 months)
3. **Permanent ban**: Egregious or repeated behavior

## Reporting

To report a violation:

1. **Email**: conduct@focalpoint.local (private)
2. **GitHub**: Report to maintainers via private message
3. **Anonymous**: Submit via [Anonymous Form](https://focalpoint.local/report)

Include:

- Description of incident
- Involved parties (if comfortable sharing)
- Any relevant links or screenshots
- Your preferred outcome

## Resolution

We will:

1. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. Investigate thoroughly
3. Communicate findings and actions
4. Respect confidentiality (for victim and reporter)

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/).

Thank you for helping create a safe, respectful community. 💙
